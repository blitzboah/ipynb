How RickRoll took over the internet

Never Gonna Give You Up song by Rick Astley released in 1987, was one of the music videos that took over the internet during 2010's era. People will add a link to the music video to just troll the user. This trolling was known as RickRoll. To this day, RickRoll is still quite relevant.
